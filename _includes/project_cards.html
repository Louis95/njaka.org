Coding and arts are some of the most valuable tools a kid can have in this century, but developing that skill and creativity takes a village. That’s why every program we offer and every workshop we run is designed to help this generation of kids set the course for a better future.

Njaka runs workshops in 3 regions in Cameroon (Southwest, Littoral, and Northwest) and expanding rapidly to other parts of the country and eventually, Africa.

At Njaka Workshops, your kids learn how to code and develop websites, apps, programs, games, and explore technology using MIT Scratch​, HTML, Javascript, Python, and more! They also learn the culture of reading and sharing through computer programming, poetry, prose, and games.
